Who is Soulja Kelly?

Soulja Kelly aka “Big Soulja” is a Ghanaian artiste making waves across the European continent. He was born and raised in Tema. His passion for music and iconic fashion sense has always set him apart from his peers. Growing up, he was largely inspired by American rap and was usually seen reciting lyrics and writing songs in his free time. His biggest inspiration is the American rapper DeAndre Cortez AKA Soulja Boy. Inspiring him to come up with his pseudonym “Soulja Kelly”. The rapper and songwriter released his first official single titled “Lil Soulja” in 2017. He continued to work on his music career while in school and released an independent project called “THE BEST” in 2019 with an official video under one of the biggest labels in Ghana – “ Ground Up Chale ”. In the summer of 2022, his infectiously abrasive single “SAY MY NAME” and 2 tapes “YOUNGEST IN CHARGE” & "FIVIE TAPE" caught the attention of many new listeners. His intro (All Love) on the “Youngest in Charge” tape featured UK’s OG rapper C. Biz which he also got co-sign by Brooklyn-based drill rapper Fivio Foreign . This tape elevated his presence in the drill scene in Europe. He immediately followed with an Afrobeat single "Santorini" which continued to build on the rapper's momentum. Soulja Kelly says he’s the “Youngest In Charge ” in Europe.
